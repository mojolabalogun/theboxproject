The Box Project

A Rice University student-led research project about labels and how they define us.

Please, take our survey about Labels!

We want to hear about what terms you use to define yourself and how that has shaped you experiences.

All responses can be entered anonymously by choosing not to enter a name or email. Non-anonymous responses will be entered into a raffle to win VIP tickets to an art gallery sponsored by The Box Project!

Take the Survey!


Feel free to take the survey again by simply refreshing the page; especially if you have multiple labels that represent your identity.
